DCA Strategy Info

Important considerations for DCA trading

How DCA Works

Trigger: Position drops below DCA threshold (e.g., -10%)
Action: Add more SOL to reduce average cost basis
Repeat: Can DCA multiple times based on max count

Risk Warnings

Increased Exposure: DCA increases total capital at risk per position
Falling Knife: DCA won't help if token continues downward trend
Cooldown: Use cooldown to avoid rapid-fire DCA entries
